About Me

I live within 10 miles of all of my family and on purpose! Before that, I also was briefly in a Social Work program but left that when deciding to become a nurse. Job wise, I have worked in liability insurance and most recently as a MedTech in an assisted living facility. My goals now are to complete this program and find a job that I can dive into and do well. My hope is to get into a position of more financial stability with better benefits and a greater emotional and mental capacity for my girls.
